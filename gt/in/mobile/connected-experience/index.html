The connected power of Samsung Galaxy

Galaxy connected devices are perfect together, opening up new ways to seamlessly communicate, work, play and stay healthy. 1

Compatibility issues are so last decade. Galaxy devices are designed to work together, so whatever you’re doing just flows naturally. From big-screen video calls and file sharing to turning health goals into health wins, it’s all easier with Galaxy.

Share almost anywhere

Forget barriers between devices. Galaxy lets you take your content where you want it to go.

Quick Share

Quick Share across Android devices

Quick Share makes sharing photos, videos and documents so fast and easy it’s almost instant. With just a few taps, you can share files with nearby Galaxy smartphone, tablet and PC — and now even with non-Samsung Android devices. You can also use Quick Share to send files to multiple contacts at once, even if they’re far away. 2

Smart View

Mirror your phone to your TV. Keep your privacy.

With App cast from Smart View, when you’re screen sharing from your phone to your TV, you can keep any personal messages from popping up on screen—and still be able to use your phone for other tasks. 3

Get in touch like never before

Open communication is easier in an open Galaxy ecosystem.

Video Call Transfer

Talk, watch and laugh together

On a video call while you're heading home to relax on the couch? Keep the conversation going by seamlessly transferring your Google Meet call from your phone to your tablet. Then on busy mornings, you can easily start your first video call of the day on your phone and transfer it to your tablet without missing a beat. 4

Call on other devices

Never miss a call

If you’re working on a Galaxy Tab or PC, your connected Galaxy phone will send any calls your way so you’re always in the loop. 5

Elevate your productivity
with Samsung Galaxy

Syncing Galaxy devices lets you create an efficient, get-things-done workstation wherever you are.

Link to Windows

What’s on your phone is
now on your PC

With Link to Windows, you can check mobile notifications and messages on your PC and respond to them instantly. Instead of having to hop between apps on a phone or tablet, you can see them on your PC screen at the same time. Plus, the Recent Apps feature will show you the mobile apps you just used—right on your PC—so you get quick access and workflow continuity between devices. You can also continue browsing from the Samsung Internet app on your phone or tablet to your PC browser by using the Recent Websites feature. 6

Link to Windows

Use your phone as a
connected camera

If you want to use the Samsung Galaxy device’s powerful camera as a PC webcam, select your phone camera on the video conferencing apps while connecting Galaxy devices with your PC via Link to Windows. Thanks to the excellent image quality, you will feel fully engaged with your colleagues at work, and you can switch freely between front and rear cameras. You can also apply a variety of features from your phone to your call – including Background Blur and Auto-framing – that help focus your image in any environment, whether working in a busy coffee shop or communicating from a company boardroom. While using your phone as a webcam, you can still reply texts, check incoming calls and use other apps, too.

Second Screen

Extend your digital workspace

Expand your workspace with Galaxy Tab. Second Screen extends your PC screen onto your Galaxy Tab, so you get the extra room to do more. It also means you can use the S Pen on your Galaxy Tab to draw illustrations, write notes and more. Plus, you can enable the Second Screen connection right from the Galaxy Book without having to touch your Galaxy Tab. 7

Samsung Notes

Collaborate anywhere with Samsung Notes

With Samsung Notes, your notes update automatically across your Galaxy devices using Samsung Cloud, for a seamless note-taking experience. You can even create a note and invite people to view and edit together. Use a link to have coworkers join in on the collaborative project. 8

Multi Control

Controlling devices is super easy

Multi Control lets you use one mouse, keyboard or trackpad to control your Galaxy Book, Tab or Phone simultaneously. The input controls of your Galaxy Book or Galaxy Tab can be used for your Galaxy devices. That means you can copy and paste text, and drag and drop images from one device to another seamlessly — almost like they're the same device. It's an easier, more seamless way to handle all your tasks. 9

Make your life smart and healthy with Samsung Galaxy services

Your Galaxy Watch and Galaxy phone work together as one powerful connected team.

Daily Activity & Fitness Tracker

Enable your everyday wellness

Your Galaxy Watch checks your health in detail and provides various tips on how to enable your everyday wellness. Better understand your sleep patterns and receive healthy sleeping guides straight from your Watch. Go through your day with peace of mind, as your Watch constantly monitors your heart health and sends a notification if it detects an irregular heart rhythm, prompting you to take an ECG for a more thorough examination.10

Body Composition

Get an instant body composition reading

Place two fingers on your Galaxy Watch, follow the steps that appear on the screen and get measurements for your Skeletal Muscle and Body Fat. Monitor your data with your Watch, and track the changes in your body composition as you work toward your fitness goals.11

Find My Phone

No need to worry about leaving your phone behind

Can’t find your phone? If it’s nearby, you can ring it through a paired Watch with Find My Phone. If it’s out of range and disconnected from Bluetooth, you can use SmartThings Find (Find My Mobile) to access your phone’s location on a map and ring it from a distance on your paired Watch. 12

Enjoy entertainment without limits

Galaxy makes it easier to share, search for and immerse yourself in your favorite music and shows.

Media Output

Keep the music playing during switches

When you want to continue listening to music on Bluetooth speakers, Buds or Wi-Fi speakers, now just open Media output on Quick panel. With just a few taps, you can conveniently control the devices on one screen. Enjoy your favorite music in any part of your home. 13

Auto Switch

Jump from movies to calls automatically

Say you’re watching a movie on your TV with your Galaxy Buds. If you get a call on your phone, Auto Switch will instantly connect your Galaxy Buds to your phone, so you can answer from your Buds —then, when you hang up, it will connect you back to your movie. Use Auto Switch freely among your Galaxy smartphone, tablet and laptop. 14
